# Clearances and Tolerances

## Fit Types

| Fit | Clearance/Side | Use |
|-----|---------------|-----|
| Press-fit (interference) | -0.05 mm | Permanent joints, dowels. Add 0.5 mm×45° lead-in chamfer. CAUTION: use tough resin only |
| Transition (snug) | 0.1 mm | Alignment pins, lids that stay put without fasteners |
| Clearance (sliding) | 0.2–0.3 mm | Removable covers, interlocking parts |
| Moving parts | 0.5 mm minimum | Hinges, sliders, rotating joints |

## Screw Clearance Holes and Counterbores (Socket Head Cap Screws)

| Screw | Clearance Hole | Counterbore Ø | Counterbore Depth |
|-------|---------------|---------------|-------------------|
| M2 | 2.4 mm | 4.4 mm | 2.0 mm |
| M2.5 | 3.0 mm | 5.4 mm | 2.5 mm |
| M3 | 3.7 mm | 6.5 mm | 3.0 mm |
| M4 | 4.8 mm | 8.25 mm | 4.0 mm |

**CRITICAL:** Always ensure clearance ABOVE screw heads — the counterbore depth equals the head height. Add 0.5 mm extra depth if the head must sit fully below the surface. If a lid or other part sits above a screw, ensure at minimum head_height + 0.5 mm gap.

## Shaft and Bore
- Rotating shaft: 0.15 mm clearance/side
- Sliding shaft: 0.1 mm clearance/side
- Orient critical bores vertically (Z-axis) for roundest holes

## PCB Clearances
- PCB-to-wall: 0.5 mm minimum, 1.0 mm preferred (1.5 mm if routing wires between)
- Below board (standoff height): 3 mm min, 5 mm standard, 8–10 mm with bottom components
- Above board: tallest component + 3 mm minimum to lid/ceiling
- Around mounting holes: 3–5 mm keep-out radius (screw stress zone)

## Port/Connector Cutouts
- Add 1.0 mm clearance per side to connector body dimensions
- Chamfer cutout edges 0.3–0.5 mm for cable insertion guidance

| Connector | Body Size | Cutout Size |
|-----------|----------|-------------|
| USB-C | 8.4×2.6 mm | 10.4×4.6 mm |
| USB-A | 12.0×4.5 mm | 14.0×6.5 mm |
| Micro-USB | 7.5×2.5 mm | 9.5×4.5 mm |
| DC barrel 5.5×2.1 | 11 mm Ø | 11.5 mm Ø |
| 3.5 mm audio | 6 mm Ø | 6.5 mm Ø |
