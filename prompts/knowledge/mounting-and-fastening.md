# Mounting and Fastening

## Screw Boss Design (Self-Tapping)

| Screw | Pilot Hole Ø | Boss OD (min) | Engagement Depth |
|-------|-------------|---------------|-----------------|
| M2 | 1.5–1.6 mm | 5.0 mm | 4.0 mm |
| M2.5 | 1.9–2.0 mm | 6.0 mm | 5.0 mm |
| M3 | 2.2–2.4 mm | 7.0 mm | 6.0 mm |
| M4 | 3.0–3.2 mm | 10.0 mm | 8.0 mm |

- Pilot hole = 75–85% of screw minor diameter
- Wall around hole: minimum 1× screw diameter
- Add 2–3 gusset ribs to each boss (50–60% of wall thickness)
- Fillet at boss-wall junction: 0.5 mm minimum

## Heat-Set Insert Boss Dimensions (Preferred for Resin)

| Insert | Insert OD | Printed Hole Ø | Boss OD (min) | Hole Depth |
|--------|----------|----------------|--------------|------------|
| M2 | 3.5 mm | 3.1 mm | 7.0 mm | insert length + 1 mm |
| M2.5 | 4.0 mm | 3.6 mm | 8.0 mm | insert length + 1 mm |
| M3 | 4.6 mm | 4.2 mm | 9.2 mm | insert length + 1 mm |

- Printed hole = insert OD minus 0.4 mm
- Boss wall: minimum 2× insert OD thickness around hole
- Install with soldering iron at 250–270°C
- **Preferred over self-tapping for resin** — resin is brittle; heat-set inserts provide reusable threads

## PCB Standoff Heights

| Height | Use Case |
|--------|----------|
| 3 mm | Minimum. Solder-side clearance only, no bottom components |
| 5 mm | Standard. Clears through-hole leads and small SMD |
| 8 mm | Bottom-side components (caps, resistors) |
| 10 mm | Bottom connectors, wire routing underneath |

**Never mount a PCB directly on an enclosure floor.**

## Snap-Fit Design (Tough/Durable Resin ONLY)

- Cantilever thickness: 1.5–2.0 mm
- Cantilever length: minimum 5× base thickness
- Hook depth: 1.2 mm (light) to 2.0 mm (secure)
- Fillet at root: radius ≥ 0.5× base thickness
- Taper: reduce thickness 40–50% from root to tip
- Lead-in angle: 20–30°, retention angle: 45–90°
- **DO NOT use snap-fits with standard resin** — it will crack on first use (2–5% elongation)
